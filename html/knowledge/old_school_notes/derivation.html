Chapter 5-3 Force


Everyday experience tells us that a given force will produce different accellerations in different bodies. Put a baseball and a bowling ball on the floor and give each a shove. The accelleration of the baseball will be much greater.

m_x = m_0{a_0 \over a_x} = {\left( 1 kg \right)}{1 m/s^2 \over 0.25 m/s^2 } = 4 kg
