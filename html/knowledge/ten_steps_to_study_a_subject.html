10 Steps to Studying a Subject

  1. Scan the required reading
  2. Read the material doing examples in the book Try to stay two chapters ahead
  3. Do the homework
  4. Ask questions about the homework, that are not understood
  5. Keep three bookmarks in textbooks
    • Answers to questions in the back
    • Place that class is at
    • Place in book that I am comfortable with material
  6. Make up flash cards on hard to learn, very important, or closely related subjects, whenever studying subjects have them close at hand and when you can answer a question run through all of them.
  7. Always try to do a little from each class each day spend extra time with class with next test
  8. ALways write dates on handouts
  9. Have 4-5 pieces of graph paper with axis in the middle and two with axis on the left side
  10. Always have a backup whenever giving a presentation
